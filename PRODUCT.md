# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
People in and around Portici who need a taxi or private transfer quickly: residents, tourists, hotel guests, families with luggage, business travelers, and visitors moving between Portici, Naples, Capodichino airport, stations, the port, Pompeii, Vesuvius, and the Amalfi Coast.

## Product Purpose
The site presents Taxi H24 Portici C.P. 25 as a direct, trustworthy local taxi and transfer service. Success means a visitor can understand the service, trust the vehicle and local driver, and start a WhatsApp, phone, or Maps action in seconds.

## Brand Personality
Fast, local, reassuring. The voice should feel direct and practical, with enough Campania character from real photography and route names to avoid feeling like a generic ride-hailing template.

## Anti-references
Avoid anonymous app-startup pages that hide the real service behind generic gradients. Avoid travel-agency clutter, fake luxury claims, placeholder car stock photos, and pages that require a visitor to hunt for a contact action.

## Design Principles
Show the actual taxi before explaining anything.
Make contact actions impossible to miss on mobile, especially WhatsApp and phone.
Use local destinations as proof of expertise.
Keep pricing language honest when exact fares are not provided.
Borrow Bolt's clarity and pace without copying its brand.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard access. The page should remain usable with reduced motion, on small phones, and for visitors who only scan headings and action labels.
